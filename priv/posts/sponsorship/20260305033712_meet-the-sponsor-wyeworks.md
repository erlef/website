Welcome to Meet the Sponsors, a section where you can learn about the organizations that support our foundation. Here you will learn about them and why they decide to support this great community. Today we will get to know WyeWorks. Enjoy!
---
<img src="/images/wyeworks.png" class="img-fluid" alt="Sponsor- WyeWorks"/>

WyeWorks is a custom software agency with a team of expert Elixir engineers that partners with startups and growing companies to build and scale high-quality web products, acting as a true extension of their teams. WyeWorks focuses on long-term impact, as evidenced by the past 17+ years helping companies across a wide range of industries, including EdTech, healthcare, e-commerce, and more.

With a strong engineering culture and a product-minded approach, WyeWorks specializes in delivering reliable, maintainable systems that support long-term business growth. 

The company is particularly known for its expertise in modern web technologies, including Elixir, Ruby on Rails and JavaScript ecosystems. What sets WyeWorks apart is its focus on collaboration, transparency, and technical excellence.

Rather than acting as a simple outsourcing vendor, the team integrates closely with clients, often functioning as an extension of their internal teams. This approach allows WyeWorks to contribute not only at the implementation level but also in architecture, product strategy, and critical technical decision-making. Headquartered in Uruguay and working with clients primarily in the US and Europe, WyeWorks combines the advantages of nearshore collaboration with a strong emphasis on craftsmanship and continuous learning. 

Its involvement in the global tech community—through conference participation, sponsorships, and open technical dialogue—reflects a long-term commitment to building software that matters and relationships that last.

#### Why the BEAM

WyeWorks uses Elixir to build highly reliable, scalable, and maintainable systems for clients where concurrency, fault tolerance, and long-term performance are critical. Leveraging the BEAM ecosystem, they help companies migrate legacy systems and design new architectures that can grow sustainably, while empowering teams with clear, expressive code and proven distributed-systems foundations. 

#### About sponsoring the foundation

WyeWorks is committed to supporting the long-term health of the BEAM ecosystem, actively sharing knowledge and engaging with the global Elixir and Erlang communities. By sponsoring the foundation, WyeWorks strengthens their commitment to helping the ecosystem grow.

For more information, have a look at their [website](https://www.wyeworks.com/ "website").

*Our foundation is supported by the funding of our sponsors, and it is what allows us to carry out projects, marketing initiatives, stipends programs, and accomplish the goals that we have established as an organization. So if you are interested in becoming a sponsor, contact us at [sponsorship@erlef.org](mailto:sponsorship@erlef.org). We look forward to hearing from you!*
